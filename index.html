About

This website is a collection of my works so that I can share them with the world. Contained is a collection of various projects I've worked on, music that I've been involved in making, and various papers I've written. This page will serve as a hub to various content I create on the interweb.

Who I Am

I enjoy creating new works and understanding our complex world. I am currently a Software Engineer for Canonical helping to make Ubuntu better. Primarily I fix bugs, but also try to make it easier to fix what counts. In addition, I've enjoyed music my entire life, and continue to play with friends and in a band locally. I also brew beer, garden, run races and read books on philosophy, economics and science fiction.
